Apart from this Privacy Policy, we offer data and privacy details integrated into our products and specific features that request the use of your personal information. This product-specific information is complemented by our Data & Privacy Icon.

What Is Personal Data at CT?

At CT Innovation, we firmly uphold the fundamental rights of privacy — and believe that these rights should remain consistent regardless of one's geographical location. This is why we classify any data pertaining to an identified or identifiable individual or data linked in any way to them as "personal data," irrespective of the individual's place of residence. Personal data includes information that directly identifies you, such as your name, as well as information that may not directly identify you but can reasonably be used for that purpose, like the serial number of your device. Data that has been aggregated is categorized as non-personal data for the purposes of this Privacy Policy.

Your Privacy Rights at CT

At CT, we honor your capacity to understand, access, rectify, transfer, limit the handling of, and erase your personal information. We have extended these privileges to our worldwide clientele, and if you opt to utilize these privacy entitlements, you hold the right not to experience bias in treatment or a reduction in the quality of service from CT. If you're asked to grant approval for the handling of your personal data by CT, you retain the authority to revoke your consent at any moment.
